## Developer News
CFP submissions for Open Source Summit India are due March 4. Submit [here](https://events.linuxfoundation.org/open-source-summit-india/program/cfp/).

The CFP for Project Opportunities (Lightning Talks and Maintainer Track) at KubeCon + CloudNativeCon India closes on March 1. Submit your proposals [here](https://events.linuxfoundation.org/kubecon-cloudnativecon-india/features-add-ons/project-opportunities).

The Kubernetes Working Group Serving has been disbanded after completing its objective of advancing AI inference workloads in Kubernetes. Ongoing efforts can transition to existing SIGs and working groups, including SIG Node, SIG Scheduling, SIG Apps, SIG Network, SIG Scalability, and WG Device Management. Congratulations to the WG members on successfully achieving their goals.

[ingress-nginx](https://github.com/kubernetes/ingress-nginx) will reach End of Life (EOL) on March 31, concluding its best-effort maintenance period.
